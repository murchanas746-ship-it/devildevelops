Social Impact

The question is whom does this project impact?

InAmigos aims to nurture life itself. The organisation open-heartedly opens its doors for people,animals and flora alike. Maintaining daily food drives in slums and orphanages, community kitchens during emergencies and festivals, curating dry-ration kits for the low-income families, sponsoring education for children and vocational training for the youth and women. Feeding vulnerable and abandoned animals and the tree plantation drive under Project Jeev and Project Prakriti, marvelous initiates to show the facets of the organisation to carry on relief and growth of people and the conservation and aid of plants and animals.

Join InAmigos in its mission to create a more equitable and inclusive world. Together, we can transform lives, inspire change, and build a brighter future.
